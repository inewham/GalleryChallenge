Gallery Challenge: Requirements

A photo gallery with the following functionality:

  • A tile based view/layout.
  • Neatly laid out in desktop and mobile.
  • When you press an image it should overlay the page with a larger version, that can then be closed.
  • Extra credit: Arrow buttons on the large view to go to next or previous.
